About Us

Welcome to Big Al's Athletics, your trusted partner for custom apparel in Indiana. For more than 20 years, we’ve proudly served local schools, businesses, and organizations with high-quality shirts designed to bring teams, events, and communities together.

Our story began in New Castle, Indiana, with a simple mission: to provide reliable, affordable, and great-looking apparel for the events that matter most. From school spirit wear to business promotions and community fundraisers, we’re here to help you bring your design ideas to life.

At Big Al’s Athletics, we understand that every shirt tells a story. That’s why we focus on quality materials, clean printing, and dependable service—so your group can look its best whether you’re cheering from the stands, representing your workplace, or celebrating a special event.

We’re more than just a T-shirt shop—we’re a local partner committed to supporting our community. Join us and discover why schools and businesses across Indiana continue to choose Big Al’s for their apparel needs.
